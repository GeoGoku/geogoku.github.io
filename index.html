I am currently an Assistant Professor of Geophysics at the China University of Petroleum (Beijing), a Research Affiliate at the National Key Laboratory of Petroleum Resources and Engineering. I graduated with a PhD in Geophysics from China University of Geosciences (Beijing) in January 2019.

My research interests include geophysics (Gravity, magnetic, complex-resistivity, seismic) and its application. I have published papers in leading journals such as the Geophysical Journal International, the Geophysics, the Computers & Geosciences, and the Remote Sensing. I am also a reviewer of several journals (e.g., Geophysics, Applied Geophysics, Journal of Applied Geophysics). I have taught courses on gravity, magnetic, electromagnetic, and scientific writing at undergraduate level. I am proficient in various coding languages such as Matlab, Python, and C. I also have advanced language skills in English.

计算地球物理、应用地球物理、固体地球物理

1. Forward Modeling & Numerical Simulation

Developing efficient algorithms for multi-physics forward modeling compatible with complex structures and complex terrain.

Designing novel signal processing techniques for non-seismic data, focusing on data reconstruction, resolution enhancement.

Research on inverse problem solving in geophysics, including seperate and joint inversion of multi-modal data, regularization techniques, and hybrid optimization algorithms.

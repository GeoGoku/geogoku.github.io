Research direction 01

Forward Modeling & Numerical Simulation

Developing efficient algorithms for multi-physics forward modeling compatible with complex structures and complex terrain.

Forward modeling of gravity anomalies based on cell mergence and parallel computing

Research direction 02

Data Processing

Designing novel signal processing techniques for non-seismic data, focusing on data reconstruction and resolution enhancement.

Gravity gradient tensors derived from radial component of gravity vector using Taylor series expansion

Research direction 03

Inversion Methods

Research on inverse problem solving in geophysics, including separate and joint inversion of multi-modal data, regularization techniques, and hybrid optimization algorithms.

Memory-efficient implementation of gravity inversion

Research direction 04

Field Applications

Translating theoretical advancements into practical solutions for resource exploration (oil/gas, minerals), groundwater monitoring, and engineering geophysics.

Modeling and Inversion of Airborne and Semi-Airborne Transient Electromagnetic Data with Inexact Transmitter and Receiver Geometries
